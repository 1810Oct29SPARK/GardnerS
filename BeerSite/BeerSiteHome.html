Directions to get to the brewpub:

  1. From the corner of Bruce B Downs Blvd and East Fletcher Ave, proceed South down Bruce B Downs Blvd.
  2. On your left will be USF campus. To your right numerous Medical Buildings.
  3. If you are not already on the West side of the road, make sure to cross. There is a pedestrian bridge over the street.
  4. Continue down Bruce B Downs Blvd until, on your right, you see iQ Apartments. Turn towards the building.
  5. Enter the building and make sure to use your 
    keychain
    to open access to the 2nd floor.
  6. Find Apt#????. Knock on the door to gain entrance.
